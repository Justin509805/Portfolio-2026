How Wet Will I Get?

How Wet Will I Get? is an app that I worked on with a classmate in duo's. The app is connected to a weather API and gives you a forecast of how wet you will get based on the weather and your destination.

It tracks your location and when the user chooses their destination, it calculates the time and distance to give you a forecast of how wet you will get based on the weather and your destination. The more it rains, the bigger protection you will see on the screen.
