StudentEvents Website

I worked with a team on StudentEvents, a website that allows students to find events on campus. We wanted to create a website that was easy to use and navigate, and that would be a valuable resource for students.

StudentEvents was created because mostly international students around campus had trouble finding new friends. We wanted to create a website that would help them connect with other students and make new friends.
